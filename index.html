About us

Meet Shaving Ken!


In 1995 whilst my sisters played with their Barbies, I would spend hours playing barber with my Shaving Ken doll.


About 15 years later, after qualifying at one of London's top barber academies, I spent four years sharpening my skills in barber shops in Camden town, Brixton and Shoreditch. I returned to my hometown Canterbury and after a further year in the trade became fortunate enough to find the perfect location to pursue my dream of opening my own shop.


'Shaving Ken' is a modern barbers in the heart of Canterbury, offering tailored gents grooming in a relaxed enviroment.
